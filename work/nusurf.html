Brief

NuSurf is a university surf club focused on building a strong community and brand. As Marketing Director, I led efforts to boost engagement, event attendance, and merchandise sales through creative social media strategies.

Roles

- Social Media Manager (2022-2025), promoted to Marketing Director.
- Led a 10+ member team to grow NuSurf's reach.
- Created content, managed Instagram, and designed merchandise.

Challenges & Strategy

Positioned NuSurf as a must-join community in a science-driven university. Key strategies:

- Instagram: High-quality visuals, engaging stories, and consistent branding.
- Merchandise: Exclusive designs driving sales and visibility.
- Events: Professional videos and interactive campaigns.

Results & Impact

- +300% Instagram engagement (2022-2025).
- Sold-out surf trips and record attendance.
- Revenue growth from merchandise sales.
