Brief

I started BatotaDoce in 2020, at the age of 16, as a clothing brand aiming to fill a gap in the market for simple yet stylish t-shirts and sweatshirts. I managed everything from product design to social media, marketing, and even shipping, growing the brand from the ground up.

Roles

- Founder & Creative Director (2020-Present)
- Managed product design, social media, and marketing.
- Handled customer experience and shipping logistics.

Challenges & Strategy

As a solo entrepreneur, the biggest challenge was managing creativity while keeping up with a demanding school schedule. I also had to find ways to build customer trust and keep them engaged month after month with new collections. Time management was key.

- Solution: Consistently releasing new collections every month and using high-quality visuals for social media.
- Solution: Paid advertisements on Instagram targeting people who love minimalist fashion.
- Solution: Focus on creating a personalized customer experience to build a loyal following.

Results & Impact

- Explosive growth in sales and engagement from 2020 to 2021, with customers returning for multiple purchases.
- Successful international campaign that helped grow the brand's visibility outside of Portugal.
- Created loyal customers who continued to purchase multiple t-shirts per year.
