Brief

KABARQUERT, a consulting company, approached me to design and develop their website from scratch. I was in charge of the front-end development, creating a dynamic, user-friendly site to showcase the company's services and provide smooth navigation for their visitors.

Roles

- Front-End Developer (Solo Project)
- Designed and developed the website from scratch.
- Ensured responsive design and user-friendly navigation.

Challenges & Strategy

The biggest challenge was managing the strict and detailed requirements the company had for the website while keeping it simple and user-friendly.

- Solution: Spent ample time upfront discussing the project and asking detailed questions to ensure I fully understood their vision.
- Solution: Maintained constant communication throughout the development process to ensure everything met their specific requirements.
- Solution: Delivered a dynamic, easy-to-navigate website with a clean, professional design.

Results & Impact

- Highly satisfied client: The company was very pleased with the outcome, and the website helped them gain more clients and traffic.
- Positive feedback: The website was well-received by both the company and its clients.
