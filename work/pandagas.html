Brief

PandaGas is a car-sharing app designed to simplify shared transportation by automatically dividing and recording all expenses, making rides more efficient, eco-friendly, and hassle-free.

Roles

- Conceptualized and designed the app.
- Developed key features like expense tracking and ride scheduling.
- Created a user-friendly interface using Figma.

Key Features

- Expense Tracking: Automatically splits fuel, tolls, and other costs among passengers in real time.
- Ride Scheduling: Intuitive interface for quick trip reservations and management.
- User-Friendly Design: Minimalist and easy-to-navigate for clarity and efficiency.

Impact

PandaGas makes car-sharing more efficient, cost-effective, and eco-friendly by reducing the need for individual car ownership.
